"IT on-demand" refers to a model of IT services and resources that are readily available and accessible whenever they are needed. In this context, "on-demand" means that IT services, such as infrastructure, software, support, or expertise, can be quickly provisioned and scaled up or down as required by a business or organization. This model offers flexibility and agility, allowing businesses to respond rapidly to changing needs without the need for large upfront investments or long-term commitments.
