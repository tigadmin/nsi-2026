How It Works

On-demand refers to a type of service or product delivery model that provides instant or near-instant access to a particular good or service when the customer needs it. The on-demand model typically uses technology, such as web or mobile apps, to facilitate the ordering, payment, and delivery of the goods or services.

For example, on-demand services might include ride-sharing companies like Uber or Lyft, which allow customers to request a ride and have a driver pick them up within minutes. Other examples of on-demand services might include food delivery, grocery delivery, house cleaning, pet sitting, or home repair services, where customers can easily order and pay for the services they need through a mobile app or website, and have the service provider dispatched to their location in a short amount of time.

On-demand services have become increasingly popular in recent years, as customers seek more convenience, speed, and flexibility in their daily lives. This model has disrupted traditional service and product delivery models and created new opportunities for businesses to meet customer needs in innovative ways.
